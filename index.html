Blood Orange Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 blood oranges
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • ½ cup cold water
  • Ice cubes

Varieties of Blood Oranges

  • Moro – Deep red, rich in flavor
  • Tarocco – Sweet and juicy
  • Sanguinello – Slightly tart with raspberry notes

Nutrition Facts (per 1 cup)

  • Calories: 80
  • Vitamin C: 90% of Daily Value
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 16g
